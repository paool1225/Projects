Final Project Proposal

Topic

My website is going to be about my Game company that I literally just created for the sake of this project. I am very interested in game development and engineering and one of the best ways to get employers to notice that is a nice little website showcasing some of my projects and artistic capabilities in both 2d and 3d art.

Audience

The audience is mainly potential employers who would like to offer internship/employment opportunities as well as customers who would like to commission me for a game.

Scope

The website will have 4 pages: a homepage, a page showcasing games I have had a part in making, a page showing off 2D art I have made, and a page showing off 3D art I have made in Maya.
